# Audit Export Assistance Agreement Excerpt

## Section 2. Support Response Windows

For Severity 1 export failures affecting quarter-close operations,
Provider will acknowledge the issue within two business hours and supply
an initial action plan within one business day.

## Section 4. Audit Support

Provider will use commercially reasonable efforts to help Customer
prepare audit-export evidence packets, but does not guarantee regulator
acceptance of any exported artifact.

## Section 6. Evidence Retention

Generated export evidence will be retained for ninety days unless the
parties approve a shorter window in writing through the customer's
designated compliance approver.

## Section 8. Exclusions

The response windows above do not apply when the failure is caused by
Customer-controlled upstream data sources, unsupported browser
automation, or manual data edits outside the documented export flow.
