# Quarterly Filing Excerpt

Reporting period: Q4 FY2025

Operational disclosure:

The company experienced a temporary increase in enterprise audit export
failures during quarter close, primarily in large evidence packages that
exceeded normal retry thresholds.

Mitigation statement:

Management introduced bounded worker backoff, clearer operator status
copy, and additional evidence-retention controls for generated export
artifacts.

Risk factor note:

Future reporting periods may still face customer dissatisfaction if
export throughput does not keep pace with quarter-close demand.
